About Me

Annabel Aikman

Speech and Language Therapist

Passionate about helping people with language difficulties, specializing in DLD (Developmental Language Disorder).

Contact: slt@aikman.ch

In Her Words

I am a highly experienced U.K. trained Speech and Language Therapist skilled at working with children aged 2 – 18 years. I am a member of The Royal College of Speech and Language Therapists and have additional degrees: BA (Hons) in English Language and Linguistics MMedSci in Clinical Communication Studies Since moving to Switzerland over twenty years ago, I switched from working with children who were mostly monolingual to those who are mostly bilingual (English plus one or more languages). I am raising my own children in a bilingual environment (English/German). This gives me a valuable insight into how best to support bilingual children and their families.
